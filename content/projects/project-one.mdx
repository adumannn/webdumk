## Overview

This initiative focused on aligning multiple product teams on a shared component library with accessibility baked in from day zero.

### Objectives

- Launch a unified design language in under eight weeks.
- Reduce UI defects by empowering teams with guardrails.

## Outcomes

The new system shipped to production in six weeks and now supports three core products. Teams report a 40% reduction in QA churn and faster iteration cycles.
